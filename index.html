Recipe #1: Egg And Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients Quantity
Eggs Two
Cheese One slice
Bread Two Slices
Stick of Butter One Little Tiny Slice

Step 1: First you have to toast your bread. To toast your bread, put the bread in a toaster until it pops out. (I don't know how to use a toaster...) If you don't have a toaster like I do, use your air fryer! Put your two slices of bread on a pan and place the pan in your air fryer.

Step 2: While your bread is toasting, place a small, round pot on the stove and set the stovetop temperature to the medium temperature (anything in-between really really hot and not cooking at all.)

Step 3: Put a very small slice of butter in the pan. Swirl it around the pan with a spatula or just by holding the handle and letting the butter hit every side of the pot, so your food won't stick.

Step 4: While your pot is heating up with the butter, crack the eggs in a small bowl and whip them up until they are scrambled.

Step 5: Pour the scrambled eggs onto the hot pan and stir with spatula until you see its all cooked. (Make sure the eggs are fully cooked, you don't want to get salmonella!)

Step 6: Scrape your eggs off the pot and put your cooked eggs on a plate so they don't burn, and turn off your stove.

Step 7: Next, take out your toasted (hopefully not burnt) toast. You may have taken your toast out before your eggs were done and thats okay, everyone has their preferences!

Step 8: Transfer your eggs into your bread slices and voila! An egg and cheese sandwich.
